About

    I've always enjoyed fixing things and finding out how they work, which is why software engineering appeals to me so much. It gives me an opportunity to break my own (or other people's) code, find out why it broke, fix it, and learn something in the process.

    Some of my main skills include being able to learn a new programming language, environment, and whole code base quickly. Some languages I've worked with and feel comfortable using include Kotlin, C#, SQL, Java, C, C++, Visual Basic, JavaScript, Python, Vue, and HTML, but I'm never opposed to taking on something new!

    Whether I'm stashing my changes to troubleshoot a higher-priority issue, setting breakpoints to understand why a variable is null, or limiting my browser's connection speed to replicate a bug, I'll use any software's many tools to help me achieve my goal more efficiently and effectively.

    I graduated from York College of Pennsylvania in May of 2021 with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.
